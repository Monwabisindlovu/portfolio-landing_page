About Us

The idea for WeatherApp stemmed from my desire to create a user-friendly platform for accessing accurate weather forecasts. As outdoor enthusiasts, we understand the importance of reliable weather information in planning various activities. This project is a culmination of our passion for coding and my interest in meteorology.
